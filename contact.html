Friends

The Friends Apartment Building

The 6 main characters in Friends all live in the same apartment building. In between scenes shots of this building are shown, and this building is based in Grenwich Village. This building also have a small cafe on the ground floor, however this isn't Central Perk as that was a set in LA.

Movie Location:
Corner of Bedford and Grove, New York City, Manhattan, New York, USA
